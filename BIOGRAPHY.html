Biography of Olive Alila


About

Olive Alila is a former student of Kabare Girls' High School.As an accomplished student she participated in various competitions including SIWI science convention and numerous scrabble competitions including the Nigerian Youth Tournament in earl August 2023.She was also a member of the prestigious science club of Kabare Girls' High School and treasure of the famous Kabare Scrabble team during her highschool life.In addition,she was also acting entertainment captain in said school since late 2022 to late 2023 when she retired after completing her National Examinations(KCSE)
